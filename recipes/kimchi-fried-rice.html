Kimchi Fried Rice

Easy and healthy Kimchi Fried Rice with smokey bacon and egg!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Kimchi
  • 4 rasher of streaky bacon
  • 200g of Enoki mushrooms (root removed, risned and drained)
  • 3 cups of steamed wholegrain rice - if it's fresh leave it out for 5-10 mins at room temperature (day old rice works best)
  • 2 large eggs per portion
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup Kimchi juice (from the bottom of the Kimichi container)
  • 1/2 Tbsp sesame oil x2
  • 1/2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp roasted sesame seeds, to garnish
  • (optional) 1/2 stalk green onion, thinly sliced

Time to cook!

  1. On medium high heat preheat a pan/wok and once heated, add one of the sesame oil and spread it well with a spatula.
  2. Add garlic to the pan and stir for 10 seconds. Next, add bacon and stir until half-cooked.
  3. Add the kimchi to the pan, stirring until itâ€™s 80% cooked.
  4. Add the mushrooms and mix them well for a few seconds. Reduce the heat to medium-medium low.
  5. Add the rice and the kimchi juice. Mix all of them together well and thoroughly.
  6. In a different pan add the cooking oil and fry and egg how you like it - I like mine runny!
  7. Add the other sesame oil and mix them well. Remove the pan from the heat.
  8. Serve the kimchi fried rice on a plate. Garnish with the sesame seeds, and green onions (all optional). Place the cooked egg on top. Enjoy!
