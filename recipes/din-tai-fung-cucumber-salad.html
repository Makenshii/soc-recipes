Din Tai Fung Cucumber Salad

A refreshing and flavourful side dish!

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Mushroom Powder (optional)
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Chilli Oil
  • Sugar
  • Warm Water

Time to cook!

  1. Cut your Persian cucumber into ¾-inch thick slices and add them to a medium bowl. Sprinkle the slices with salt and toss to combine
  2. While the cucumber is resting in the salt, slice your garlic cloves into thin slices.
  3. Pour warm water into a small bowl filled with the sugar and stir the water and sugar together until the sugar is completely dissolved.

    4. HINT!:

    If using the mushroom powder (which is optional), you can add it to the bowl and stir it with the sugar and water.

  4. Then, slice the bell pepper. Then, chop the bell pepper (skin side) into ¼-inch wide pieces.

    5. PRO TIP!

    Taste the salted cucumber after it’s been sitting for 25 minutes and adjust based on your preferences. If you find that it’s a bit too salty for you, you can rinse the salted cucumber slices and pat them dry before dressing.

  5. Plate the cucumber and top them with the sugar water mixture. Add the sliced garlic and red bell pepper.
  6. Dress the cucumber salad with sesame oil and chili oil.
